# TalentCopilot AI

TalentCopilot is an AI-powered Recruitment Intelligence Platform.

Its goal is not only to rank candidates, but to help recruiters understand why a candidate matches a role, what risks should be explored, and what questions should be asked during interviews.

## Product principles

- Explain every recommendation
- Show evidence from CVs
- Support multilingual recruitment: English, French, Mandarin
- Use a capability-based assessment model
- Keep the recruiter in control of the final decision
