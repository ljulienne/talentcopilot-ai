## AI Decision Queue

This release converts explainable Organization Insights into a human-controlled decision queue.

### Added

- `AIDecision`, `DecisionQueue`, priority, status and effort models.
- `DecisionEngine` converting recommendations into ranked decisions.
- Do now / Plan / Monitor prioritization.
- Evidence and source-insight traceability.
- Decision queue Streamlit experience.
- CSV export.

### Governance principle

TalentCopilot proposes decisions. A human remains accountable for accepting, executing or dismissing them.
