# TalentCopilot Release 1.1 — Skills Intelligence Engine

## Business objective

Turn employee skill exports into an explainable skills portfolio that identifies rare capabilities, strategic gaps and uneven departmental coverage.

## Delivered

- Multilingual skill normalization and aliases.
- Skill categories.
- Rarity and coverage scoring.
- Strategic skill gap analysis.
- Department coverage analysis.
- Skills insights compatible with Executive Intelligence and the AI Decision Queue.
- CSV export and Streamlit integration.

## Boundaries

This version analyzes declared skills from uploaded HR data. It does not infer proficiency from private communications or protected personal attributes.
