# Release 7.1.3 — Evidence Prioritization & Fluent Advisory Writing

## Objective
Raise Recruitment Workspace narratives to consultant-grade quality without changing official scores, rankings, risk levels, or recommendations.

## Changes
- Separates eligibility evidence from differentiating evidence.
- Prevents tenure from being selected as the strongest evidence by default.
- Prioritizes projects, transformations, tools, ownership, operating scope, and measurable impact.
- Removes robotic labels such as `Tool Sap` from visible prose.
- Reduces repetition of score, recommendation, and confidence across narrative paragraphs.
- Produces fluent executive summaries, candidate assessments, and recruiter reasoning.
- Preserves deterministic scoring introduced in Release 7.0.1.

## Validation
- New focused tests cover evidence ordering, fluency, de-duplication, terminology cleanup, and non-regression.
- Full test suite passed on the Release 7.1.2 snapshot at HEAD `24e8f7d2030a8188b03720adcd34eaf2c78c492a`.
