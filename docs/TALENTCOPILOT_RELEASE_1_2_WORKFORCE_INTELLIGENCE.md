# TalentCopilot Release 1.2 — Workforce Intelligence Foundation

## Purpose

Simulate the business continuity impact of an employee departure using existing organization, skills and knowledge metadata.

## Delivered

- Departure scenario engine.
- Critical and unique skill exposure.
- Explainable continuity risk score.
- Internal successor ranking.
- Workforce insights compatible with Executive Brief and Decision Queue.
- Successor CSV export.
- Streamlit integration in Organization Intelligence.
