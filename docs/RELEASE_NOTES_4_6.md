# TalentCopilot-AI Release 4.6 — Executive Decision Center

## Purpose
Release 4.6 adds a decision-readiness layer above the canonical recruitment outputs. It measures whether a decision is sufficiently documented; it does not rescore candidates.

## New capabilities
- Decision Readiness score and unresolved evidence gaps.
- Evidence Quality classification: Direct, Partial, Weak inference.
- Explainable canonical AI confidence.
- Executive onboarding timeline from Week 1 to Month 6.
- Structured peer trade-off view based on existing official scores and skill evidence.
- Executive Decision Report PDF v2.

## Governance
Official Match, official rank and canonical AI confidence remain read-only. The new readiness score evaluates evidence completeness only and must not be interpreted as candidate quality or used autonomously.
