# Release 4.7 — Interview Intelligence Pro

## Purpose

Complete the evidence-led interview workflow of the Recruitment module while preserving the Blueprint separation between official matching and interview decision support.

## Added

- Targeted Interview Playbook display.
- Live recruiter evidence capture by competency.
- Explainable STAR completeness analysis.
- Dynamic follow-up prompts for missing context, ownership, action, results and metrics.
- Evidence-confirmed competency scorecard.
- Explainable Strong Hire / Hire / Borderline / No Hire recommendation.
- Remaining evidence and risk detection.
- Downloadable Interview Intelligence PDF report.

## Governance guardrails

Release 4.7 does not recalculate or mutate:

- official matching score;
- official candidate rank;
- AI confidence.

The recommendation is based only on recruiter-entered interview ratings and evidence confirmation.
