# Release 7.1 — Consultant-Grade Recruitment Intelligence

## Objective

Raise recruitment analysis, recommendations and interview preparation from rule-output lists to professional, evidence-led decision advice while preserving deterministic official scores.

## Changes

- Professional multi-paragraph candidate narratives.
- Explicit distinction between missing CV evidence and a confirmed capability gap.
- Contextual risk calibration: mid-range fit is not automatically labelled High risk.
- Candidate-specific, decision-oriented interview validation priorities.
- Interview question objectives now state the exact uncertainty to resolve and the expected ownership/outcome evidence.
- Existing official score and rank calculations remain unchanged.

## Guardrails

- No candidate-specific scoring rules.
- No LLM dependency added to official scoring.
- Risk and narrative outputs remain deterministic.
