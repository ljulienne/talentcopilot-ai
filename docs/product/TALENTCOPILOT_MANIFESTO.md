# TalentCopilot Manifesto

## We are not building an ATS

TalentCopilot does not aim to replace applicant tracking systems.

## We are not building an HRIS

TalentCopilot does not aim to become the system of record for employees.

## We are building an AI diagnostic layer for HR and organizations

TalentCopilot helps leaders understand what is hard to see: weak signals, decision risks, collaboration gaps, hidden expertise and organizational blind spots.

## Product principles

1. Explain before scoring.
2. Tell the decision story before showing metrics.
3. Always connect recommendations to evidence.
4. Prefer focused diagnostics over generic dashboards.
5. Use uploads first, integrations later.
6. Keep existing HR systems as systems of record.
7. Build insights that ATS and HRIS tools do not naturally provide.
