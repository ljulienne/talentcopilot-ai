# Interview Workspace

The Interview Workspace helps recruiters prepare structured interviews.

It answers one business question:

> Are we ready to interview this candidate, and what must we validate?

## Sections

- Candidate Snapshot
- Interview Readiness
- Competency Matrix
- Evidence Coverage
- Interview Plan
- AI Questions
- Live Notes
- Interview Scorecard
- Decision Readiness

## Future integration

This workspace is designed to later consume `CandidateDecisionProfile` generated by the Decision Intelligence Core.
