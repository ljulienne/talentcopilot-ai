# Hiring Budget Workspace

The Hiring Budget Workspace helps recruiters evaluate whether a candidate is financially feasible.

## Rule

Budget Fit must not change Candidate Fit.

A candidate can be:

- excellent fit but over budget;
- weak fit but affordable;
- acceptable fit and financially feasible.

## Outputs

- budget fit;
- salary gap;
- cost impact;
- feasibility;
- recommendation;
- next actions.
