# TalentCopilot Enterprise — Product Blueprint v1.0

## Vision

TalentCopilot Enterprise is an Explainable AI Talent Intelligence Platform.

Its mission is to help organizations make faster, clearer and evidence-based hiring decisions.

## Product Principles

1. AI assists. Humans decide.
2. Every AI recommendation must be explainable.
3. One workspace answers one business question.
4. Each persona receives the right level of information.
5. Evidence is more important than opaque scoring.
6. The product must be demo-ready and enterprise credible.

## Personas

### Recruiter
Needs to coordinate the recruitment process, analyze candidates and prepare interviews.

### Hiring Manager
Needs to assess whether a candidate will succeed operationally in the team.

### HR Director
Needs a reliable, explainable and defensible decision summary.

### HRIS Administrator
Needs system quality, integrations, logs and governance.

### Interview Panel
Needs structured feedback and consistent evaluation support.
