# Interview Evaluation & Scorecards

This module turns interview notes and competency ratings into a structured post-interview evaluation.

## Purpose

The evaluation answers:

> Did the interview increase or decrease decision readiness?

## Outputs

- competency ratings;
- overall interview score;
- decision impact;
- strengths confirmed;
- risks remaining;
- recommendation after interview;
- exportable summary.

## Future integration

In Release 2.0, these outputs will feed `CandidateDecisionProfile` and the Decision Intelligence Core.
