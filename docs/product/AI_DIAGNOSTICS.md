# AI Diagnostics

TalentCopilot Next is organized around diagnostics rather than pages.

## Recruitment Diagnostic

Question: did we find the right candidates, and what should be verified before deciding?

## Candidate Diagnostic

Question: should this person move forward, and what evidence supports the decision?

## Interview Diagnostic

Question: what must be challenged during the interview?

## Organization Diagnostic

Question: where are the invisible silos, hidden connectors and collaboration risks?

## Skills Diagnostic

Question: which capabilities will become critical or scarce?

## Transformation Diagnostic

Question: what organizational risks could slow down change?
