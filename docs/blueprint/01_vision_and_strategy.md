# 01 — Vision & Strategy

## Product Vision

TalentCopilot is an AI Hiring Decision Platform.

It helps organizations make faster, fairer and more explainable recruitment decisions by combining candidate evidence, structured evaluation, budget feasibility, interview insights and collaborative decision governance.

## Product positioning

TalentCopilot should not be positioned as:

- a traditional ATS;
- a simple CV matching tool;
- an opaque candidate scoring engine.

TalentCopilot should be positioned as:

> An Enterprise Hiring Decision Intelligence Platform.

## Core promise

TalentCopilot helps hiring teams answer:

> What is the best recruitment decision, and why?

## Strategic differentiation

Most recruitment tools answer:

> Who matches the job description?

TalentCopilot answers:

> Which hiring decision is most defensible, feasible and evidence-based?

## Target users

- Recruiters
- Hiring Managers
- HR Directors
- Talent Acquisition Leaders
- HRIS / People Analytics teams
- Executives involved in strategic hiring decisions

## North Star

Every major TalentCopilot output must improve decision quality.
