# 25 — Evidence Gap & Interview Intelligence

## Purpose

Interview Intelligence converts missing evidence and risk signals into targeted interview questions.

It answers:

> What should the recruiter validate next?

## Inputs

- CandidateDecisionProfile
- Decision Trace
- Fit signals
- Risk signals
- Confidence signals
- Budget signals

## Outputs

- Evidence gaps
- Interview questions
- Expected strong answer
- Positive signals
- Red flags
- Follow-up prompts
- Evaluation criteria

## Design rules

1. Interview questions must be connected to decision gaps.
2. Interview Intelligence does not change Fit Score.
3. Interview Intelligence prepares validation; it does not decide.
4. Questions should be explainable and linked to the candidate profile.
