# 02 — Product Principles

## Principle 1 — AI assists, humans decide

TalentCopilot never replaces the recruiter, hiring manager or HR leader.

It provides recommendations, evidence, risks and confidence levels.  
The final decision remains human.

## Principle 2 — No score without evidence

Every score must be supported by evidence.

A score without evidence creates false confidence.

## Principle 3 — No recommendation without confidence

Every recommendation must include a confidence level.

A strong recommendation with low confidence should trigger further validation.

## Principle 4 — Fit and feasibility are separate

Candidate Fit must never be reduced because a candidate is expensive.

Budget feasibility is a separate dimension.

## Principle 5 — Every decision must be traceable

TalentCopilot must preserve a Decision Trace showing how evidence, scores, risks and recommendations were produced.

## Principle 6 — Workspaces answer business questions

Every workspace must answer one clear business question.

Examples:

- Recruitment Workspace: Where is this recruitment?
- Candidate Workspace: Who is this candidate?
- Interview Workspace: What must we validate?
- Hiring Budget: Can we afford this hire?
- Decision Board: Are we ready to decide?
- Executive Reporting: How do we justify the decision?

## Principle 7 — Enterprise trust over black-box automation

TalentCopilot optimizes for trust, transparency and explainability, not automatic decision-making.
