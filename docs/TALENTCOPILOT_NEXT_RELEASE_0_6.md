# TalentCopilot Next — Release 0.6

## Collaboration Intelligence Foundation

This release turns the existing organization graph into an explainable collaboration diagnostic.

### Capabilities

- Overall collaboration health and score.
- Department-level collaboration metrics.
- Strong silo signal detection.
- Weak department-pair detection, including zero-link pairs.
- Critical collaboration bridge identification.
- Standardized Organization Insights and recommendations.
- Streamlit experience and CSV export.

### Important limitation

The graph is inferred from HR export metadata: reporting lines, shared skills and backup relationships. It is not a full communication ONA and must not be presented as direct observation of email, meeting or messaging behavior.
