# ADR-007 — Budget Intelligence in Decision Core

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Hiring decisions are not only about candidate fit. Financial feasibility matters.

However, mixing budget into Fit Score makes the score misleading.

## Decision

Introduce Budget Intelligence as an independent Decision Core engine.

## Consequences

- Budget feasibility is explicit.
- Compensation trade-offs are visible.
- Recommendation Engine can combine fit and feasibility later.
