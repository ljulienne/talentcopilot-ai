# ADR-006 — Risk Intelligence is separate from Fit Intelligence

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Risk and fit are different concepts.

A candidate may fit the job but still carry risk because of missing evidence, weak confidence, budget uncertainty or critical gaps.

## Decision

Risk Intelligence produces independent risk outputs and does not modify Fit Score.

## Consequences

- Workspaces can explain trade-offs clearly.
- Recommendation Engine can combine dimensions explicitly.
- Recruiters can see whether a candidate is weak or simply risky.
