# ADR-017 — RoleProfile as job-side contract

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Decision Core requires role requirements in structured form.

Manual dictionaries are not enough for real recruitment flows.

## Decision

Introduce `RoleProfile` as the structured output of Job Intelligence.

## Consequences

- Real job descriptions can feed Decision Core.
- Matching becomes less dependent on demo data.
- Future Role Graph can evolve from RoleProfile.
