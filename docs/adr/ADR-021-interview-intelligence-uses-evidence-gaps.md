# ADR-021 — Interview Intelligence uses evidence gaps

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Generic interview questions are less useful than targeted validation questions.

Decision Core already identifies fit, risk, confidence and evidence signals.

## Decision

Interview Intelligence should generate questions from CandidateDecisionProfile gaps and risks.

## Consequences

- Interviews become structured and evidence-based.
- Recruiters can validate uncertainty directly.
- The Decision Trace can explain why each question was suggested.
